ERA ExposureExposure and risk management

A unified risk score for each asset: vulnerabilities, host criticality, active detections, and configuration signals. Helps the CISO answer "what to fix first" — not by raw CVSS, but by real exposure in your perimeter.

Purpose

Reduce alert noise and focus remediation on what is truly dangerous to the business. Especially important with fleets of thousands of hosts.

Key capabilities

  • Asset risk score based on vulnerabilities, detections, and criticality.
  • "Top risky hosts" report for leadership and the VM team.
  • Linked to ERA Vuln and ERA Core security events.

Who it's for

CISO, VM/CTEM teams, banks requiring measurable risk reduction.

Platform integration

Runs on top of ERA Core and ERA Vuln: without the vulnerability scanner and XDR telemetry, the exposure picture will be incomplete.
