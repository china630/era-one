Deployment model

One platform — two deployment profiles. Data, data lake, AI, and investigations always remain in your perimeter; outbound traffic is limited to licenses, protection updates, and support telemetry (only if you allow it).

CriteriaERA Sovereign (air-gap)ERA Sovereign Hybrid
Data, data lake, AI, casesIn customer perimeterIn customer perimeter
Rule / CVE / AI pack updatesOffline package (removable media)Automatic from ERA cloud
Licensing and supportOffline keyOnline subscription + offline fallback

Hybrid mode is enabled deliberately and is configurable: raw data, personal information, and investigation materials never leave the perimeter in either mode.
