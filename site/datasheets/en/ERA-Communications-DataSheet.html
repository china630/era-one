ERA CommunicationsSovereign enterprise communications

A complete on-prem replacement for Microsoft Exchange, CommuniGate Pro, and IceWarp — email, calendar, chat, and video meetings within the customer perimeter. Backend on Rust + Go; integration with the unified ERA One identity layer and ERA Control SIEM. Data and Air-Gap AI never leave the perimeter.

Editions

ERA Mail Server
Mail backend, calendar, Autodiscover for mass deployment.
ERA Mail Client
Webmail and desktop/mobile clients; Zero-Touch migration.
ERA Conference
On-prem video meetings (LiveKit), 1000+ participants.
ERA Chat
Enterprise messenger; integration with mail and Conference.
ERA Comms AI
Air-Gap LLM: mail security audit, correspondence summaries.
ERA Mail Connect
Migration bridge: webmail + BFF to external IMAP/JMAP/EWS.

Killer features

  • Target scale of 60,000+ concurrent users without degradation.
  • Air-Gap AI — local LLM for mail; data never leaves the perimeter.
  • Zero-Touch migration — Autodiscover for Outlook and mobile clients.
  • Zero hardware lock-in — x86 and PowerPC builds.
  • DevSecOps — Distroless containers; audit in ClickHouse; integration with ERA Control.

Technical stack

  • Core: Rust + Go; ClickHouse audit storage.
  • Video conferencing: LiveKit on-prem.
  • Shared SaaS: identity, tenant, admin portal (ADR-0024).

Documents, spreadsheets, and presentations are in the separate ERA Office product, not in Communications.
