ERA CommunicationsSovereign enterprise communications

A complete on-prem replacement for Microsoft Exchange, CommuniGate Pro, and IceWarp — email, calendar, chat, and video meetings within the customer perimeter. Integrated native stack (Rust + Go) — not Postfix + Dovecot. Outlook and mobile parity via server-side Autodiscover, EWS, CalDAV, CardDAV and ActiveSync — no desktop connector plugin.

Gov-ready protocol stack

  • Autodiscover + EWS — Outlook desktop sees Exchange; mail, calendar, contacts, notes/tasks (documented subset).
  • CalDAV + CardDAV — open standards for calendar and contacts.
  • ActiveSync subset — mobile sync like Exchange (iOS/Android).
  • SMTP/IMAP + TLS — Thunderbird and standard clients.
  • No MAPI, no IceWarp-style Outlook Connector — server façade only.

Killer features

  • Target scale of 60,000+ concurrent users without degradation.
  • Air-Gap AI — local LLM for mail; data never leaves the perimeter.
  • Zero-Touch migration — Autodiscover for Outlook and mobile clients.
  • Zero hardware lock-in — x86 and PowerPC builds.
  • DevSecOps — Distroless containers; audit in ClickHouse; integration with ERA Control.

Technical stack

  • Core: integrated Rust mail + Go protocol adapters (Stalwart-class; native ERA code).
  • Video conferencing: LiveKit on-prem.
  • Shared SaaS: identity, tenant, admin portal (ADR-0024).

Documents, spreadsheets, and presentations are in the separate ERA Office product, not in Communications.
