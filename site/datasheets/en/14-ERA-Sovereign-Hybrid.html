ERA Sovereign HybridData at home — support like the cloud

A modern operating model without losing sovereignty: the platform, data lake, AI, and investigation materials remain in your perimeter, while protection updates, licenses, and support telemetry are delivered from the ERA cloud over an outbound channel. The answer to "we need on-prem, but without the cloud we fall behind in operations."

How it works

Agents› Data lake · AI · Cases — in customer perimeter› Relay (outbound only)› ERA cloud: licenses · updates · support

Hybrid mode is enabled deliberately and configured by policy. Fully isolated mode (air-gap) remains available with no outbound connections whatsoever.
