ERA Manage (UEM)IT-Ops and fleet management

Fleet management from a single perimeter: inventory and CMDB, financial ITAM (contracts, software licenses, TCO), deployment and patching, application and USB control, BitLocker, and server protection before OS patches ship (Virtual Patching). One ERA agent — no separate heavy UEM client per function.

Purpose

Consolidate IT and security in air-gap: one platform and one agent instead of multiple separate products.

Key capabilities

  • ITAM/CMDB: hardware, software, serial numbers, asset owners.
  • Financial ITAM: contracts, licenses, total cost of ownership.
  • Deployment and patching via local mirror in the perimeter.
  • Application Control and USB device blocking by policy.
  • Centralized BitLocker and recovery key management.
  • Virtual Patching — risk reduction before OS patch installation.

Who it's for

Organizations with thousands of endpoints, government and banking with UEM in a closed perimeter.

Licensing

Separate ERA Manage license; often bundled in ERA IT-Ops with Service and Provision.
