ERA VulnVulnerability management

In-perimeter vulnerability scanner: match installed software against CVE databases, scan schedules, and credentialed checks. Results link to ERA Core asset inventory and underpin risk prioritization (ERA Exposure).

Purpose

Meet VM / CTEM requirements in air-gap without cloud scanners. Provides a unified "what is vulnerable" view across the ERA agent fleet.

Key capabilities

  • Match installed software against CVE databases.
  • Scan schedules and policies.
  • Credentialed scan for deep software inventory.
  • Link to asset inventory and CISO reporting.

Who it's for

Customers with VM tender requirements, banks and industry with a separate vulnerability perimeter.
