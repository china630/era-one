Bottom line: BigFix excels at patch/compliance and large legacy estates. ERA Control wins where organizations need a unified sovereign stack: patching and UEM plus XDR, SOAR and AI in one agent, with no phone-home. Sovereign Hybrid addresses the “cloud convenience” argument without moving data or investigations outside the perimeter.
